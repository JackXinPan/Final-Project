Surface Water TTHM Prediction Model Performance Comparison

The best model for prediction of TTHMs in surface water was trained using Gradient Boosting Regression. For optimal performance all measurements should be entered for prediction however Dose Rate (mg/L), UVA and FOC (mg/L) can be used with minimal reduction in accuracy.

Ground Water TTHM Prediction Model Performance Comparison

The best model for prediction of TTHMs in ground water was training using Random Forest Regression and grid search cross validation to tune hyperparameters. All parameters are required for prediction.
